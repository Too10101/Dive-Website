How Points are Scored

At most meets there are 3 to 5 judges. Each judge gives a score from 1 to 10. Then after the judges give there score the highest and lowest score are taken away (Only of the judges scores are taken).

How Skills are Valued

Point vallues for skills are determined by the skills difficulty. Difficulty is decided by the amount of flips and twists that are in the dive.
